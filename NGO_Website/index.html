A LITTLE ABOUT OUR NGO

Our Aim : Imagine yourself out to a market shopping, enjoying, relishing your fond of items & suddenly you are informed of a heavy fire breaking out at your home. Helter & skelter, knocking & rising you somehow reach the spot.But how would you tackle such a heavy downpour situation all alone. Perhaps you would beg for help, look out for some water or sand or above all surrender yourself to the fate of firefighters. But what if all the aforesaid explored avenues go unheeded. You simply can’t stand to watch out the fire engulf your beloved sweet home. Metaphorically speaking, the burning house can be visualized as our SMVDU ecosystem & its dwellers & the fire be envisaged as the medical harakiri that engulfed one of our dear member of the family last year & eventually had cost India an avid & budding talent.
Can we all afford to behold such fires reducing to ashes our near & dear ones ? Can we all accept losing the latest talents & all life of ill treatment? Can we all do absolutely nothing except lamenting ? Not at all.

To quote the reports of WHO, every year about 152 million people loose their life in dearth of proper treatment, every year 4 of the ten Indians can’t afford the medical expenses to cure, with an equal number of men, women & children succumbing their lives to a worth nought . And all credits to our damn junks, devastated & dilapidated Medical (HealthCare) Security System. Let’s get back to where we started, the fire after all can be blown out timely & evidently if you had three basic systems. First, some self stacked fire extinguishers at your very own home. Secondly. Some really helping neighbours, kith and kin too make your work lighter. Thirdly, a commercial channel to immediately seek the firefighter’s support.
This is what we are doing at Handsforhelp. We're all creating that system of firefighting if not fireproof, the system where any member of the SMVDU ecosystem is ensured proper medication. We're all leveraging a platform to ensure every kid a safe and carinf surrounding around them. We won't wait for the mishaps sabotage a life and all because of Red Tape oe delayed relief.
